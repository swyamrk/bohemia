Bohemia

The Creator of Punjabi Rap
BOHEMIA aka Raja aka Roger David famous for creating Punjabi Rap scene is a Pakistani Punjabi rapper and music producer from California.

    Exploring Bohemia's life:

  • 15 October 1979 Born in Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan
  • He moved to United States with his family when he was 13. He started learning music at a very young age from his father. He started writing Punjabi poetry around then as well.
  • Bohemia was at America much opposite of palm trees and green grass. The town was infested with drugs and violence. After he lost his mother to cancer, he left home and survived as a local musician
  • In early2000 moved to Oakland to join his cousin working at a West Oakland recording studio. There he met Sha One (Seth Agress) and made plans to form own record label, "The Outfit Entertainment".
  • Vich Pardeshan De (In the Foreign Land)
    Bohemia's debut album in 2002 containing an autobiographical story of his life as a Desi youngster adopting the streets of America. He and Sha One produced together under the label "The Outfit Entertainment".The first single Sha Te Ra was released as a music video but the song itself was not included in the album.
    The Album was hitting Top 10 on BBC Radio UK.
  • Pesa Nasha Pyar [Money, Intoxication, Love]
    First full-length Punjabi rap album released by major label. It was recorded and produced Sha One and Bohemia in their downtown Oakland studio. After that Bohemia got a multi-record deal with Universal Music [2006-2009].In 2006, Pesa Nasha Pyar was #2 on Maxim magazine's Top 10 downloads, and #3 on Planet M Chart India. Bohemia appears in XXL magazine New York.
  • Bollywood Bohemia enters Bollywood with the title track for Warner Bros. film 'Chandni Chowk to China', appearing in the film with Akshay Kumar and Deepika Padukone.
    He lends his voice for the film 'Desi Boyz' to set the scene for Sanjay Dutt's cameo.
  • Da Rap Star His album released in 2009 got nominations at the UK Asian music awards as 'Best International Album' and PTC Punjabi music awards for 'Best Punjabi Album', 'Best Sound Recording' and 'Best Music Director' in the year 2010. The album also remains #1 on Plane M chart several weeks.
    Bohemia was one of the best-selling artists of the year according to UMG India in 2009.
  • PTC Punjabi Music Awards 2013
    Thousand Thoughts released under Sony Music India got Best International Album at PTC Punjabi Awards.
  • Coke Studio Pakistan
    In 2012 Bohemia became the first Rap artist invited to the legendary Coke Studio Pakistan. He played three songs "School Di Kitab", "Paisay Da Nasha", and "Kandyaari Dhol Geet"
  • 2015
    Award of Excellence Music at FOG Festival Of Globe and Make it large Award for Music Career at Mirchi Music Awards Punjabi.
  • PTC Punjabi Music Awards 2016
    Jaguar one of the life changing songs of Sukh-E Muzical Doctorz with Bohemia and Patola which helps Guru Randhawa became an overnight star were nominated for Best Duo at PTC Punjabi Music Awards 2016.
    Patola clinched the Award.
  • Kali Denali Music
    Bohemia launched his own music label/production house called Kali Denali Music i.e. KDM and has since been active introsucing new music scenes.
  • In 2016 Bohemia was signed with India's largest music company, T-Series.
  • Skull & Bones-The Final Chapter
    In February 2017 Skull & Bones-The Final Chapter released. The album is completely written, recorded, and composed by himself. The album sparked rumors and controversy about Bohemia's alledged connection with Illuminati, mostly due to CD cover, also designed by Bohemia himself.
    The album hit the #1 on iTunes Charts and had a total of 10 tracks.
    Classics like ‘Bijlee’ narrates a tragic tale of a girl next-door dealing with peer pressure and today’s society. In ‘Zamana Jali’ he raps and shares his views on the world, media and politics of today. With ‘Cadillac’ and ‘Meri Jeet’ he gets back to his classic yet fresh west-coat style beats and spitfire flow. With ‘Gol Gol’, ‘Nazere Mili’, ‘Titli’ and ‘Lak Da Hulara’ we get some fusion of hip-hop and melodic R&B style. And finaly ‘Photo’ in which he updates his fans about his journey thus far.
  • Skull & Bones: Volume 2 is an upcoming album of which Umeed has been released which takes us to true Bohemian fashion of story telling.
  • KDM Mixtape Volume 1 was released under Kali Denali Music in the year 2016.
